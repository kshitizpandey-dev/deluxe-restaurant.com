Walk in for the tandoor. Stay for the whole spread.

Deluxe Family Restaurant sits in Ahmedabad as a full family hall — air-conditioned, halal, and built around a menu wide enough that nobody at the table has to compromise. Punjabi gravies sit beside Indo-Chinese wok dishes, tandoori grills beside falooda and milkshakes.

No beef on the menu, halal throughout, and a kitchen that runs late into the night for the after-dinner crowd and the home-delivery order alike.
